Types of Investments

Cryptocurrency

A cryptocurrency is a collection of binary data which is designed to work as a medium of exchange where in a computerized database using strong cryptography to secure transaction records, to control the creation of additional coins, and to verify the transfer of coin ownership.

Mutual Funds

A mutual fund is a type of financial vehicle made up of a pool of money collected from many investors to invest in securities like stocks, bonds, money market instruments, and other assets.These Mutual funds are operated by professional money managers.
